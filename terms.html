1. Program Name & Description

The Memo is a family communication device and messaging service. Parents and guardians send SMS text messages to a dedicated phone number assigned to their Memo device at home. The device displays these messages and reads them aloud for children. Children can reply by recording voice messages on the device, which are transcribed to text and delivered back to the parent's mobile phone via SMS.

2. Consent to Receive Messages

By creating a Memo account and registering your mobile phone number during device setup at thememohome.com, you expressly consent to receive SMS messages from The Memo service at the phone number you provide. These messages include transcribed voice messages from your children, delivery confirmations, and service notifications related to your Memo device.

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your family's usage of The Memo device. You will receive an SMS each time your child records and sends a voice message from the device, as well as occasional service notifications. There is no fixed number of messages per month — frequency depends entirely on how often your family uses the device.
