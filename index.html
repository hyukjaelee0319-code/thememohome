Family Communication, Simplified

The answering machine for the text era

The Memo is a home device that lets parents text their kids — and lets kids talk back. No smartphone required.

How it works

1

Parents text

Send an SMS to your Memo's number, just like texting anyone else.

2

Kids hear & read

The Memo displays the message on screen and reads it aloud.

3

Kids reply by voice

Children tap to record a message. It's transcribed and texted back to you.
